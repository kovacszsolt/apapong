How to Play

Learn how to master apaPong

This is apaPong - a classic Pong game for two players. Master the controls and become the champion!
Objective

Hit the ball to your opponent's side to score points.

First player to reach 5 points wins the game.

Controls
W Left Player: Move Up
S Left Player: Move Down
↑ Right Player: Move Up
↓ Right Player: Move Down
Gameplay

The ball gets faster with each hit

Countdown before each new round

Sound plays when the ball bounces

Use strategy and timing to outplay your opponent

Settings

Customize the ball color to your preference

Choose your favorite paddle colors

Personalize player names

During Game

ESC: Return to main menu

R: Restart game (after game over)
