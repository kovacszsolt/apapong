Privacy Policy

1. General Information

This privacy policy describes the data handling practices of the apaPong game (hereinafter: "Service"). By using the Service, you accept this policy.

2. Data We Collect

The Service handles the following data:

  • Player Names: Names entered by players (maximum 8 characters)
  • Color Settings: Paddle colors chosen by players (RGB values)
  • Ball Color: Ball color selected by the user (RGB values)

Important: This data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage) and is never sent to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage Location and Method

All data is stored in your browser's local storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never sent to external servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Does not include personally identifiable information

4. Data Deletion

To delete your data, simply clear your browser's localStorage data, or use your browser's "Clear browsing data" function. After deleting the data, the game will reset to default settings.

5. Cookies

The Service does not use cookies. We use only the browser's localStorage function for data storage.

6. Third Parties

The Service uses the Kaplay JavaScript library for game functionality, which is loaded from a CDN (unpkg.com). This library does not handle personal data.

7. Children's Data Protection

Since the Service does not collect personally identifiable information, the provisions of GDPR Article 8 regarding children do not apply.

8. Your Rights

Since the Service does not handle personally identifiable information and does not send data to external servers, the rights provided by GDPR (access, deletion, correction, etc.) can be exercised through your browser's localStorage function.

9. Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of changes when the page is updated.

10. Contact

For questions regarding data handling, please contact us through the GitHub repository Issues section.
